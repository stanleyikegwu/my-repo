SmartHydro Features

Hydration Tracker

Monitors your daily water intake and helps you stay on track with your hydration goals.

Reminders & Alerts

Gives gentle reminders to drink water at regular intervals via smart alerts.

Bluetooth Sync

Syncs with your smartphone to log hydration data and provide real-time updates.

Temperature Display

Shows real-time temperature of the water inside so you know if it's hot or cold.
